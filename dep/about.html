About TechFloor

TechFloor is a Student Organization that is devoted to the development of Computer Applications that have the potential to make the campus a better place and advance members experience with program languages and technologies. Any student with an interest in technology, specifically digital technology, is welcome to join our group. Our group has a diverse set of interests such as web site development, graphic design, gaming, digital photography, digital film, database applications, PC maintenance and repair, networking, and much more. TechFloor is more than just a group of Computer Science and Information Science Majors because projects involve more than just software engineering. Areas needed for upcoming projects include Public Relations, Advertising, Statistical Analysis, Graphic Arts, Drawing, Programming, Network Management, Database Management and Digital Video Editing.

We are in need of:

  • Art and Graphic Design Majors
  • Computer Science Majors
  • Information Science Majors
  • Business Majors
  • Communication Majors
  • Economics Majors
  • Psychology Majors
